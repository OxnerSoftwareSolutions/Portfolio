About Benji

I am a software engineer living in Atlanta, Georgia. After graduating North Georgia Technical College for welding, I worked for someone else for around 15 years. It's been something that I have loved and enjoyed for most of my life. As I was working in the sheet metal industry I started to play around with some of the computers. The whole time I was on the computer I was having as much fun as I was in the shop welding on things. It was after I had started my own business as a welder that I decided to step out and start my career as a Software Engineer.

As a student at Thinkful, I've begun my journey to becoming a Software Developer and I couldn't be happier. I'm really happy with my progress and know that there is still much to offer and much to do.

If you're looking to hire a new developer please reach out! I'm so excited to work with other developers to build great software
